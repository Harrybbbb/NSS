WHO WE ARE

At the National Service Scheme (NSS), we are dedicated to fostering a spirit of social responsibility and community service among students across India. Established in 1969, the NSS is a vibrant community of young volunteers committed to making a positive impact in society.We envision a society where individuals are actively engaged in service, fostering a culture of empathy, solidarity, and sustainable development.
