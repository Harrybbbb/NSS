NOT ME, BUT YOU

The National Service Scheme (NSS) at IET DAVV, guided by the motto "Not Me But You," focuses on selfless service and community welfare. Recognized as a Tobacco Free Educational Institution by the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare and WHO, we also assist SSLC students at Government Higher Secondary School in Semmenchery through evening classes, achieving over 90% board exam success. NSS IET DAVV fosters responsible citizenship and societal well-being through dedicated volunteer efforts.

About Us

Discover Our Mission

NSS sparks student commitment to social welfare, ensuring aid reaches those in need. Volunteers glean insights from communities, fostering empathy. In crises, NSS provides essential support, embodying hope for a resilient society.

Our Vision

The NSS program fosters students' commitment to social welfare and dedication to serving society. Volunteers assist those in need, enhancing quality of life and preserving dignity while gaining insights from rural communities. NSS provides hope and support during disasters, emphasizing its commitment to a more empathetic and resilient society.
